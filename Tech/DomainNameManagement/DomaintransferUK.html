In order to transfer a .UK domain to Tucows, the domain owner must first contact the current Registrar and ask them to change the IPS Tag to “TUCOWS-CA“.  If the current Registrar is unable or unwilling to make the change, Customer need to contact Nominet directly and they will help make the change.

More information about transfers and changes in ownership for .UK domains is available directly from Nominet:

https://www.nominet.uk/
