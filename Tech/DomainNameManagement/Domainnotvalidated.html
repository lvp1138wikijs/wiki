If a Domain name was not validated by customer and got suspended

In such a situation, customer needs to approve the validation email sent to domain's contact email address. It is important that they keep their domain's contact email address up to date. Every year, Opensrs will send a validation email to the email address to make sure it is valid.

 

If the 'contact email' is not accessible, then an SMS message can be sent to the mobile device to get this domain name validated.

 

In some cases, both contact email and phone number validation is not possible. Then we can update the contact email from Reseller Opensrs panel (https://manage.opensrs.com).
