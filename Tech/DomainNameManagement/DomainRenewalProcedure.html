For Active Accounts

Reply:

You can simply renew domain from Server Point client portal. Use the following procedure

1. Go to https://www.serverpointapps.com. Then click on Client Portal.

2. Click on the Menu , then go Domain names and you will see a list of your domains and expiration dates.

3. Click on the domain name you want to renew.

4. Click on the "Action" Tab >> Then click to "Renew" button to renew the domain. Domain name can be renewed for a maximum of 9 years of renewal

The payments will be processed from the credit card or from the account funds.

(If the domain is past 37 days renewal date, it doesn't show up anymore in the control panel)

 

For canceled accounts

Domain can be renewed using the New ClientPortal even though the account is already cancelled and there is no active hosting account. You can use the same steps mentioned above for Active Accounts.

Your domain is in redemption period. Several alerts were sent but we never hear from you.

It has now passed its expiry period and now fallen in redemption. If your domain enters such period, the registrar will require a $120 payment to recover the domain.

Redemption period is the Grace Period of the domain for 30 days. Whois information (name, address, telephone numbers, etc.) will be deleted. The domain will still be inactive. The domain may be renewed by it's original owner for a very high fee. It is a manual process for the registry to add the name/address information back into the domain registry records, therefore, the process may take several days.

Five days after the end of the redemption period the domain name will be deleted from the registry and will be made available for anyone to register.

Remember that decisions made by the domain's registrar, the company that handles your domain on the global Internet, is not the responsibility of your host.
