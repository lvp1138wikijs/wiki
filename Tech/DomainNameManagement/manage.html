We are one of the re-seller customer of OpenSRS http://www.opensrs.com/

Customer can change/update whois details, Nameservers of their domains from the opensrs domain management control panel https://manage.opensrs.net/. The login credentials in the welcome mail that they have received while signing up to access domain management interface.

The domain should be LOCKED automatically after register a new domain name from client portal. For any change on whois, then the domain needs to be unlocked. Domain can be Unlocked from Client portal now (Previously it had to be done by Minerva).

Resend OpenSRS login Details

  • Login to Client Portal
  • Under Domain name >> Click on the domain name you want to send OpenSrs login details
  • Click on the "Action" tab >> then click on Password Button --> Resend Password. The OpenSrs login details will be send to the domain contact email address.

Change WhoIS Details

If a customer want to change/update whois details, then ask them to provide exact details for change/update. Then do the following steps.

  • Unlock the domain from Client Portal and get login details from customer.
  • Login to https://manage.opensrs.net/ and update contact details from there.
  • Please login to Log in top https://manage.opensrs.net using your domain name (without www), username and password.
  • Along the top of the page that appears after a successful login, you’ll find links to change your organization, admin, billing or technical contact. Choose the contact whose information you would like to change, and click on the appropriate link.
  • The “Edit information” page will appear. Just change the information in the fields as necessary. If you need to change the same information in the other contacts (billing, etc), scroll down to the bottom of the page where you see “also apply these changes to” and click “yes” in the radio buttons that are beside the other contacts.
  • After you’ve finished. Click “Save information”
  • NB: Before changing the information, you need to Unlock the domain from Client Portal .
  • Login to Client PortaL
  • Under Domain name >> Click on the domain name you want to Unlock
  • Click on the "Action" tab >> then click on Lock/Unlock
  • Once the changes are saved, please make sure you LOCK domain again.
  • Then update customer and confirm changes.
  • Once customer confirmed, then LOCK domain again.

Update NameServers

If a customer want to change/update whois details, then ask them to provide new nameserver details. Then do the following steps.

  • Unlock the domain from Client Portal and get login details from customer.
  • Login to https://manage.opensrs.net/ » Click on Nameservers. Then you will get an option to update nameservers details.
  • Then update customer and confirm changes also inform if he don't want keep hosting account with us, then submit cancellation to avoid further charges.
  • Once customer confirmed, then LOCK domain again.
