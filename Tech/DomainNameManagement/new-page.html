You can simply register new domain from Server Point client portal. Use the following procedure:

1. Go to https://www.serverpointapps.com. Then click on Client Portal.

2. Click on the Menu , then go Domain names and click on New domain/transfer.

3. Then click on Register domain and Check domain availability

4. If the domain is available for register then you will get a new form enter your details.

5. Complete the form with necessary details and submit for register it.

Registration process will complete soon and once the registration process completed then the domain will list in the domain names.
