In order to manage nameservers of a domain, please follow the steps mentioned below:

1) Login to the Domain Management Portal "https://manage.opensrs.net/".

2) Click on Nameservers.

3) Add/change/remove the nameservers there.

4) Click on Save Configuration.

  • To replace a nameserver, simply edit the existing hostname.
  • To remove a nameserver, simply cleanup the existing hostname.
  • To add a nameserver, simply fill an empty field for hostname.
