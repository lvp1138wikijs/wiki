## Billing Cycle Change on Shared Main / Resold Account


```
Hi,

You can change the pre-payment settings from the control panel . Please follow the procedure


1). Login to Client Portal from https://www.serverpointapps.com/
2). Go to Menu Â» Accounting
3). Click on the Account's Domain.
4). Under information tab Choose your Billing Cycle and click on Tick sign button to SAVE settings


Please try this.  This procedure will help you to set pre-payment upto 48 months and billing cycle changes will take effect on your next payment day.

```
## Advance Payment on Shared Main Account / Resold accounts


```
Hi,

Here is the procedure to add payment to your account, please follow steps


1) Open browser in your computer, and go to https://www.serverpointapps.com/ and click on "Client Portal"
2) Once you login, click on Menu >> Accounting.
3) Click on the Account's Domain.
4) Under Payment tab Choose your Pay in advance amount and click on Tick sign button to SAVE settings.


Please let us know if you have any further questions.
```
