Billing 

At ServerPoint.Com we have developed an in-house automate billing system that will charge all accounts on their due date. No intervention is needed from customer service department except when automate messages are received instructing us to perform certain task like cancelling an specific server etc.

Payment Methods

All charges are shown in US Dollars. The following payment methods are accepted:

  • Credit Card (VISA, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • Paypal
  • Western Union
  • Bank WireTransfer (Special request Fees applies)
  • Check, Certified Checks or Money Order (Payment of 3 month is suggested)

 

Billing Cycle

Billing cycles are simply how often you pay for your hosting services. Our billing cycle begins when the customer receive an e-mail confirming that your website has become available for access over the Internet. Services are payable in advance, including any set up or extra fee, and all payments are due on the account Next Payment Date (NPD).  

Recurring charges will be accumulated until the account in cancelled in accordance with the cancellation policy.
