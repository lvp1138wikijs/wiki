# How to install an SSL certificate? - Plesk 9

```
1. Login to Plesk.
1. From the main menu, click Domains.
1. Select the domain name you want to install on.
1. Under Additional Tools, click the SSL Certificates.
1. Now you can request a CSR and upload your certificate attachments (provided by your SSL issuer). 
1. Alternatively, you can paste the certificate codes instead of uploading the files.
```
