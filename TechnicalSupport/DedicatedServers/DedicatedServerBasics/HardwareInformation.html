Details specific to Colossus machines

Colossus servers are all SuperMicro server, with redundant power supplies.

Capacity for two physical Xeon processors, with up to 4 cores each.

16 slots of memory, supporting up to 64GBs of RAM.

Colossus I servers support up to 4 SATA drives in them, and Colossus II supports 8 drives. There are Colossus servers available with up to 16 drives in them, but they are not on the web site. However, if a customer needs one, it's a custom order.

All include RAID cards.
