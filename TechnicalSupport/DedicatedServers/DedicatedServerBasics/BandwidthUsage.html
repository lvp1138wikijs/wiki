Here the procedure to see the current bandwidth usage of server.

  1. Login to the "Client Portal" from https://www.serverpointapps.com/
  2. Click Menu >> Bare Metal Servers
  3. You can find the URL to the "public bandwidth graphs page" here.
  4. To find more details MRTG graph of the server, Click the <server name> listed under "Your active dedicated servers" then click the "Bandwidth" tab.
