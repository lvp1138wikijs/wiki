While adding a new site in VirtualMin/WebMin, you can get the following quota error.

In order to avoid this error, please do the following steps:

  1. Login to VirtualMin/Webmin and then select System Settings -> Virtualmin Configuration, and set "Set quotas for domain and mail users?" to "No".
  2. You can disable them altogether on your filesystem.
  3. You can do that in Webmin -> System -> Disk Quotas. On that screen, you'll have the option of disabling quotas for any particular partition on your server.
