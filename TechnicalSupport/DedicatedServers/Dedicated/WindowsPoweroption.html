If any customer reports that his dedicated server performance is low or any benchmark results are not satisfactory or the Clock Speed is not correct, we need to  just change the power plan to High Performance. 

By default, In Windows Power option the plan is set as Balanced or Power Saver. 

In this power plan, the Clock speed will be lower than provided (normally 1.9Ghz). 

But once we change the Power plan to high performance the Clock speed will increase to 3.5Ghz
