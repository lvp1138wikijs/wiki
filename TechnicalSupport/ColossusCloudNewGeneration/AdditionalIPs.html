Login to "Client Portal" at http://ServerpointAPPs.com

  1. Click on Virtual Machines >> Manage your VMs
  2. Click on the Settings button at the right end of the VM for which you want to add IPs.
  3. Click on "Info/IPs" tab in the Settings window, there you can find "Add IP address" option
  4. Enter the number of IPs you want to add, then click on Add IP numbers button.
