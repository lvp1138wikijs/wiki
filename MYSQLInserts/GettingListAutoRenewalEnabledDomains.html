Here is the inserts for getting the list of domains which enabled auto renewal feature

select username,domainregistered,expdate,sc_free from registered_domains where status = "A" and expdate  >"2017-6-01" and expdate < "2017-9-31" and username != "minervag" and arenew = "1" order by expdate;
